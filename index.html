Impact of AmeriCorps on RI Communities

AmeriCorps offers pathways to success for both service recipients and service volunteers in Rhode Island. Working mainly in economically disadvantaged urban neighborhoods, AmeriCorps members provide vital services for those in need. AmeriCorps members who come from these same neighborhoods gain education and employment opportunities they may not otherwise have had.

Sources: RIOPC, RI DLT, RIDE, RI OPC, NSC June 2024

Educational Outcomes of Rhode Island Youth Involved with Foster Care

In the complex landscape of educational and professional journeys, youth involved in foster care navigate unique challenges that often influence their postsecondary and workforce outcomes. This report explores postsecondary enrollment, persistence, and completion as well as the subsequent employment patterns for Rhode Island youth involved with foster care.

Sources: RIOPC, RI DLT, RIDE, RI OPC, NSC April 2024
