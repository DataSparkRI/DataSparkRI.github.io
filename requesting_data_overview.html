How can I work with the RIDataHUB?

Requests/Overview content

There are three main ways to work with the RI DataHUB.

  • Partner with DataSpark to complete an analysis.
  • Request aggregated, suppressed data.
  • Apply for access to individual-level, de-identified data.

Data use requires the permission of the individual state agencies who own the data. DataSpark can help you navigate the process of obtaining authorization to use DataHUB data.

Individual-Level Data Requests

A Data Use Agreement is required for any individual-level, de-identified data request to ensure that data are used in accordance with state and federal law. Once Data Use Agreements have been executed and contracts established, DataSpark will begin preparing your data. Legal documents and data preparation can take up to 6 months to execute, depending on the complexity of the request and DataSparkâ€™s current project queue.

Aggregate-Level Data Requests

No Data Use Agreement needed, although agency review of outgoing aggregate data is part of the process. The data preparation could take up to 2 weeks to complete, potentially longer depending on the DataSpark current queue.
